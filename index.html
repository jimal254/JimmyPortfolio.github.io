How I Think

Data Strategy
Principles

Three beliefs that shape every project I take on.

01
Clarity before complexity.
A dashboard no one understands is a liability, not an asset. I design for the decision-maker, not the data scientist. If a CFO can't read it in 10 seconds, it needs to change.
02
Outcomes over outputs.
Delivering 50 reports is not success. Changing one business decision that generates revenue is. I measure my work by what changed ‚Äî not what I built.
03
Trust is the product.
When executives stop questioning the numbers and start using them to act, that's the real deliverable. Accurate, consistent, well-governed data builds organisational trust. That's what I'm building.
